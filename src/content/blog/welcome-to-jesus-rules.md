It's been a journey getting here.

When I first started competing professionally in beach volleyball, people asked me what the end goal was. An Olympic medal? A career in the sport? Those things matter — but they were never the *why*.

The why has always been the Gospel.

## Why Jesus Rules Exists

Christians are often taught to separate from the world. To keep their heads down, avoid "secular" spaces, and play it safe. But that's not what Scripture teaches. Jesus told us to be *in* the world. To be salt and light. To go into all nations.

For me, "all nations" happens to include the AVP tour, the FIVB Beach Pro Tour, and every beach in between.

Jesus Rules Ministries was built on a conviction: **if you want people to listen, you need to earn the right to speak.** And the way you earn that right is by being excellent at what you do. Not mediocre. Not "good enough." Excellent.

When you compete at the highest level, doors open. People want to know what drives you. And that's when you share the Gospel — not from a soapbox, but from the sand.

## What's Coming in 2026

This is going to be our biggest year yet:

- **~20 professional tournaments** across the AVP and FIVB tours
- **Weekly discipleship training** in Orange County (2-3x/week)
- **International outreach events** — churches and organizations hosting us for volleyball clinics and Gospel preaching
- **Documentary production** — capturing the ministry's story on film

We're also pursuing something that once felt impossible: **the 2028 LA Olympics.** My partner Dave Wieczorek and I are currently ranked in the top 20 nationally as a team, and we're putting everything on the line to qualify.
